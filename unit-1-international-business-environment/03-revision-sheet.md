# Unit 1 — Quick Revision & Mnemonics

## 1. Mnemonic: PESTLE
- **P**: Political (government stability)
- **E**: Economic (exchange rates, GDP)
- **S**: Social (cultural norms, demographics)
- **T**: Technological (AI adoption, logistics tech)
- **L**: Legal (employment laws, IP protection)
- **E**: Environmental (sustainability, green dot packaging)

## 2. Topper's Exam Booster Points
- When writing about cultural environment, always reference **Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions** to score maximum marks.
- Highlight the **Appellate Body Paralysis** when discussing legal trade dispute environments.
