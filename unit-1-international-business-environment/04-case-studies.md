# Unit 1 — Solved Case Studies

## Case Study: McDonald's Menu Localization in India
- **Problem**: Beef burgers violated Hindu beliefs; pork violated Islamic beliefs. 80%+ of consumers had religious restrictions.
- **Solution**: Removed beef and pork. Developed local favorites (McAloo Tikki, Maharaja Mac). Established a local supply chain.
- **PESTLE Alignment**: Successfully adapted to the **Social/Cultural** environment.
