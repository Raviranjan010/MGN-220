# Unit 1 — Exam Preparation (Questions & MCQs)

## 1. Solved Scenario-Based Question (LPU Exam Style - 10 Marks)
> **Scenario**: *Veda Organics, an Indian herbal cosmetics brand, wants to expand into Germany. German consumers are highly environmentally conscious (Social) and demand certified organic ingredients (Legal) and recyclable glass packaging (Environmental).*
> 
> **Question**: Analyze the PESTLE challenges Veda Organics will face and recommend how they should adapt their business mix.
> 
> **Topper's Answer**:
> - **Social Environment**: Germany has high environmental awareness. *Adaptation*: Clearly label organic credentials and avoid chemical preservatives.
> - **Legal Environment**: Strict EU Cosmetics Regulation and Packaging Waste Laws. *Adaptation*: Ensure 100% compliant documentation and use recyclable glass.
> - **Economic Environment**: High purchasing power. *Adaptation*: Position as a premium luxury organic brand.
> - **Conclusion**: Success requires localized adaptation of packaging and product ingredients rather than standardizing the Indian product line.
