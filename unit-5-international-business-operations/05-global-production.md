# Unit 5 — Global Production

## 1. Concepts
Concentrated vs Decentralized manufacturing based on value-to-weight ratio and country factors.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A company manufactures semiconductors (high value-to-weight ratio) and cement (low value-to-weight ratio).*
> **Question**: Where should they locate production for each?
> **Answer**: Semiconductors: **Concentrated production** in a high-tech country (e.g., Taiwan) and ship globally. Cement: **Decentralized production** near local markets to minimize heavy transport costs.
