# Unit 5 — Countertrade Modes

## 1. Concepts
Trade without cash: Barter, Counterpurchase, Offset, Switch Trading, Buyback.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *An Indian defense manufacturer sells helicopters to a country lacking foreign exchange. The buyer country offers crude oil instead of cash.*
> **Question**: Identify this mode and its benefits.
> **Answer**: This is **Barter** (or counterpurchase if separate transactions are used). Benefit: Facilitates trade when currency conversion is restricted or foreign reserves are low.
