# Unit 6 — Global Human Resource Management (GHRM)

## 1. Concepts
Staffing choices (Ethnocentric, Polycentric, Geocentric) and expatriate management.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *An MNC appoints an American CEO to run its Japanese subsidiary. The CEO struggles with communication and offends local labor union leaders, leading to strikes.*
> **Question**: Identify the staffing policy and discuss the failure.
> **Answer**: The firm used an **Ethnocentric staffing policy**. The CEO suffered from cultural myopia and lacked training in Japanese business etiquette (such as Nemawashi or collective decision-making). Mitigation: Shift to a **Polycentric policy** (hiring a Japanese CEO) or provide intensive cultural adjustment training before deployment.
