# Unit 6 — Global Marketing Strategy (4Ps)

## 1. Concepts
Standardization vs Adaptation in Product, Price, Place, and Promotion.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *An American soft drink company launches in the Middle East. It uses the same English slogans and imagery showing people wearing winter clothing.*
> **Question**: Criticize this campaign and recommend changes.
> **Answer**: The campaign fails due to lack of cultural adaptation. Slogans should be translated and localized (Promotion), and imagery should show local contexts and models wearing modest clothing matching Middle Eastern cultural norms.
