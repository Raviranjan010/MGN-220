# Unit-Wise Revision Cheat Sheet

## Unit 1: International Business Environment
- **Globalization**: Market integration (selling worldwide) vs Production integration (sourcing parts globally).
- **PESTLE**: External factors (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental).
- **Social/Culture**: Hofstede's Dimensions (Power Distance, Individualism, Masculinity, Uncertainty Avoidance, Long-term Orientation).
- **AI Application**: Predictive logistics, automated legal checks, automated translation.

## Unit 2: International Trade
- **Mercantilism**: Goal is Trade Surplus (Exports > Imports), accumulating gold/silver.
- **Absolute Advantage**: Adam Smith (absolute efficiency in production).
- **Comparative Advantage**: David Ricardo (lowest opportunity cost).
- **Factor Mobility**: Movement of capital and labor across borders.
- **Regional Economic Integration**: FTA -> Customs Union -> Common Market -> Economic Union -> Political Union.

## Unit 3: Global Monetary System
- **Forex Functions**: Currency conversion and hedging (using forward/futures contracts).
- **IMS History**: Gold Standard -> Bretton Woods System (fixed rate) -> Floating Exchange Rate.
- **IMF**: Short-term balance-of-payments stabilization.
- **World Bank**: Long-term economic development and poverty reduction.
- **ADB**: Social development loans in Asia-Pacific.

## Unit 4: Strategy and Structure
- **Pressures Matrix**: Cost Reduction vs Local Responsiveness (GLIT: Global Standardization, Localization, International, Transnational).
- **Entry Modes**: Exporting, Turnkey, Licensing, Franchising, Joint Ventures, Wholly Owned Subsidiaries.
- **Organization Structure**: Centralized vs Decentralized, Functional, Product Division, Worldwide Area, Matrix.

## Unit 5: International Business Operations
- **Export Documents**: Bill of Lading (receipt/contract/title), Letter of Credit (bank payment guarantee), Commercial Invoice.
- **Countertrade**: Barter, Counterpurchase, Offset, Buyback.
- **Make-or-Buy**: Make (protect IP, control quality) vs Buy (leverage supplier scale, reduce risk).
- **Logistics**: Ocean (cheap, bulk), Air (fast, high value).

## Unit 6: Global Marketing and HRM
- **Marketing 4Ps**: Product/Price/Place/Promotion - Standardization vs Adaptation.
- **GHRM Staffing**: Ethnocentric (PCNs run subsidiary), Polycentric (HCNs run subsidiary), Geocentric (best managers hired regardless of nationality).
