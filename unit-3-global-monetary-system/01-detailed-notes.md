# Unit 3 — Global Monetary System: Detailed Notes

## Topic: Foreign Exchange Market
- **Forex Functions**: Currency conversion, hedging, and speculation.
- **Hedging Instruments**: Forward contracts, futures, options, swaps.

## Topic: International Monetary System
- **Gold Standard**: Pegging currencies to gold.
- **Bretton Woods System**: Pegging currencies to USD, backed by gold. Created IMF and World Bank.
- **Floating vs. Fixed exchange rates**.

## Topic: Financial Institutions
- **IMF**: Short-term balance-of-payments crisis lending.
- **World Bank**: Long-term developmental loans.
- **ADB**: Asian development projects.
