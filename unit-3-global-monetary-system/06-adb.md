# Unit 3 — Asian Development Bank (ADB)

## 1. Concepts
ADB is a regional development bank focused on social and economic development in Asia-Pacific.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A regional corridor link project between India, Myanmar, and Thailand requires funding.*
> **Question**: What role does the ADB play here?
> **Answer**: ADB provides regional developmental loans, technical assistance, and co-financing options, promoting regional integration and economic corridors.
