# Unit 3 — International Monetary Fund (IMF)

## 1. Concepts
IMF maintains global monetary stability, providing short-term loans to countries facing balance-of-payments crises.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *Sri Lanka faces depletion of its foreign reserves and default on sovereign debt. It requests a bailout from the IMF.*
> **Question**: What conditions does the IMF typically attach to such structural adjustment loans?
> **Answer**: Structural adjustment conditions include **fiscal austerity** (spending cuts), tax reforms, privatization of state assets, and deregulation of trade.
