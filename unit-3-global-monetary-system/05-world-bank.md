# Unit 3 — World Bank Group

## 1. Concepts
World Bank provides long-term loans for infrastructure, health, and education development to eradicate poverty.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A developing nation wants to build a nationwide solar power grid to reduce reliance on coal.*
> **Question**: Which international institution should they approach: IMF or World Bank?
> **Answer**: They should approach the **World Bank** (specifically IBRD or IDA), as this is a long-term developmental infrastructure project. The IMF is for short-term balance-of-payments stability.
