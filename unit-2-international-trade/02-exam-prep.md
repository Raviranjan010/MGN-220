# Unit 2 — Exam Prep & Solved Scenarios

## Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *Country A takes 5 hours to make a phone and 10 hours for a rug. Country B takes 10 hours for a phone and 5 hours for a rug.*
> **Question**: Apply Adam Smith's Absolute Advantage theory to show how trade benefits both.
> **Answer**: Country A has an absolute advantage in phones (5 < 10 hours); Country B has it in rugs (5 < 10 hours). They should specialize, trade, and increase total output.
