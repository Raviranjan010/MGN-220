# Unit 2 — Theory of Absolute Advantage

## 1. Concepts
Proposed by Adam Smith, a country has an absolute advantage when it is more efficient at producing a good than any other nation.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *Country A takes 5 hours to make a phone and 10 hours for a rug. Country B takes 10 hours for a phone and 5 hours for a rug.*
> **Question**: Explain how they should trade.
> **Answer**: Country A has an absolute advantage in phones; Country B in rugs. They should specialize in their respective advantages and trade, increasing total output.
