# Unit 2 — World Trade Organization (WTO)

## 1. Concepts
WTO enforces rules for global trade, operates on Most Favored Nation (MFN) and National Treatment principles.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *India bans imports of solar panels from the US to support its domestic manufacturers.*
> **Question**: Analyze the WTO compliance of this move.
> **Answer**: This violates the **National Treatment principle** (treating imports less favorably than domestic goods). The US can file a dispute with the WTO Dispute Settlement Body.
