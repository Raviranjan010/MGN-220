# Unit 2 — Regional Economic Integration

## 1. Concepts
Agreements among countries in a geographic region to reduce tariff barriers.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *Three neighboring countries remove internal tariffs and establish a common external tariff. One country wants to restrict labor movement.*
> **Question**: Identify the integration level and explain the conflict.
> **Answer**: They have established a **Customs Union**. Free labor movement is only required at the **Common Market** level. The restriction is legally permissible at the Customs Union level.
