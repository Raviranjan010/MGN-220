# Unit 2 — European Union (EU)

## 1. Concepts
The EU is an Economic Union with a single market, common currency (Euro), and harmonized regulatory frameworks.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A British exporter faces new customs checks and tariffs when shipping goods to France after Brexit.*
> **Question**: Explain this change in regional integration.
> **Answer**: Brexit represented a shift from an Economic Union to a basic trade relationship, re-introducing trade friction, custom clearance paperwork, and potential tariff barriers.
