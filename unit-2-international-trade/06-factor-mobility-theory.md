# Unit 2 — Factor Mobility Theory

## 1. Concepts
Addresses the movement of labor and capital across borders.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A shortage of software engineers in Germany leads the government to ease visa restrictions for Indian IT professionals.*
> **Question**: How does this factor mobility affect both economies?
> **Answer**: Germany gains skilled labor, reducing wage pressures. India experiences a "brain drain" but benefits from increased inward remittance inflows.
