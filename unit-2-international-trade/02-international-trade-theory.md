# Unit 2 — International Trade Theory Overview

## 1. Concepts
Trade theories explain why countries trade and how trade patterns develop.

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *A country is self-sufficient but wants to protect its domestic steel industry from foreign competition by imposing tariffs.*
> **Question**: Evaluate this policy using free trade principles.
> **Answer**: While protectionism helps domestic producers in the short run, trade theories show it reduces consumer welfare, leads to resource misallocation, and risks retaliatory tariffs.
