# Textbooks & Reference Materials for MGN220

These are the primary academic resources mapped to the Lovely Professional University curriculum guidelines.

---

## 1. Prescribed Core Textbooks
- **Title**: *International Business: Competing in the Global Marketplace*
  - **Authors**: Charles W. L. Hill & Arun Kumar Jain
  - **Publisher**: McGraw Hill Education
  - **Syllabus Mapping**: Units 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. This is the primary book used by LPU examiners to set questions.
  - **Chapters to Focus on**:
    - Chapter 2 & 3: Political, Legal, and Economic environments.
    - Chapter 6: International Trade Theories.
    - Chapter 9: Regional Economic Integration.
    - Chapter 11 & 12: Foreign Exchange and Global Monetary System.
    - Chapter 13 & 14: Strategy and Entry Modes.
    - Chapter 17 & 18: Global Production, Logistics, and GHRM.

---

## 2. Recommended Reference Books
- **Title**: *International Business*
  - **Author**: Rakesh Mohan Joshi
  - **Publisher**: Oxford University Press
  - **Key Use Case**: Ideal for localized Indian trade examples, export/import documentation procedures (Unit 5), and tariff/non-tariff barrier concepts.
