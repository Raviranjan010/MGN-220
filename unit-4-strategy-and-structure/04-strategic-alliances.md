# Unit 4 — Strategic Alliances

## 1. Concepts
Cooperative agreements between competitors (alliances, joint ventures).

## 2. Solved Scenario-Based Question
> **Scenario**: *Two global automakers partner to develop electric batteries but fear technology theft.*
> **Question**: How can they structure the alliance to mitigate this risk?
> **Answer**: Use **structural safeguards** (walling off critical R&D departments), sign strict non-disclosure contracts, and make swap agreements where both contribute equal value to ensure mutual dependence.
