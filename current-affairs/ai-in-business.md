# Artificial Intelligence in International Business

## 1. Global Supply Chain Optimization
- **Predictive Logistics**: AI models analyze shipping data, weather, and custom delays to dynamically reroute cargo ships, reducing transit times.
- **Inventory Management**: Forecasting demand across different regional warehouses using machine learning models to prevent stockouts.

## 2. International Marketing & Customer Experience
- **Hyper-Localized Content**: AI translations adapt idioms and cultural slogans instantly for marketing materials.
- **Chatbots**: 24/7 multilingual Level 1 customer support, decreasing operational costs in foreign units.
