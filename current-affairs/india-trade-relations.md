# India's International Trade Relations & FTAs

## 1. Major Bilateral Trade Agreements
- **India-UAE CEPA**: Signed in 2022, removing tariffs on over 90% of trade, boosting exports of gems, jewelry, and textiles.
- **India-Australia ECTA**: Signed in 2022, providing duty-free access for Indian goods (textiles, leather, machinery) in Australia.

## 2. Key Government Initiatives
- **PLI Scheme (Production Linked Incentive)**: Subsidizing domestic manufacturing (electronics, APIs, textiles) to reduce import dependence.
- **RoDTEP Scheme**: Reimbursing duties and taxes on exported products, making Indian exports price-competitive globally.
