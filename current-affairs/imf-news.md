# IMF News & Global Financial Stability Reports

## 1. Global Sovereign Debt Distress
- **Crisis**: Over 60% of low-income countries are currently in or at high risk of debt distress. High interest rates in developed nations (like the US Fed rate hikes) have led to capital outflows from emerging markets, increasing borrowing costs.
- **IMF Interventions**: Recent bailouts for **Sri Lanka** ($3 billion), **Pakistan** ($3 billion), and **Argentina** ($44 billion) containing strict structural conditions.

## 2. Policy Actions
- Focus on restoring price stability (tackling global inflation) while maintaining financial system resilience through targeted liquidity backstops.
