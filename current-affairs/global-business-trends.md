# Global Business Trends: Reshoring and Nearshoring

## 1. Shift from Globalization to Regionalization
- **Reshoring**: Bringing manufacturing back to the home country (e.g. US semiconductor manufacturing incentives under the CHIPS Act).
- **Nearshoring**: Transferring business operations to a nearby country with geographic proximity (e.g. US firms shifting assembly from China to Mexico).
- **Friendlyshoring**: Sourcing from countries that share common political values to minimize geopolitical risks.

## 2. Drivers of the Shift
- Geopolitical tensions (US-China trade war).
- Supply chain disruptions experienced during lockdowns.
- Rising shipping container costs and logistics delays.
