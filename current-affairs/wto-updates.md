# WTO Current Updates & Trade Disputes

## 1. 13th Ministerial Conference (MC13) Key Decisions
- **E-commerce Moratorium**: Member nations agreed to extend the moratorium on custom duties on electronic transmissions (software, music, ebooks) until MC14 or March 2026.
- **Dispute Settlement Reform**: Member nations committed to having a fully functional dispute settlement system (solving the Appellate Body paralysis crisis) operating by the end of the next cycle.

## 2. The Appellate Body Crisis
- **Background**: The WTO Appellate Body (its supreme court for trade disputes) has been non-functional since late 2019 because the US has blocked the appointment of new judges, complaining of judicial overreach.
- **Impact**: Countries can now "appeal into a void," meaning they can appeal WTO panel rulings, but since no Appellate Body exists, the case remains unresolved.
