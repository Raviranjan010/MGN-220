# Top 50 Solved Academic Questions

### Q1. Explain the three main types of currency exposure (Transaction, Translation, Economic).
**Answer**:
1. **Transaction Exposure**: The risk that exchange rate fluctuations will affect the home currency value of pre-existing contractual cash flows (e.g. unpaid invoice).
2. **Translation Exposure**: The risk that consolidated financial statements will fluctuate due to foreign asset translation back to the home currency.
3. **Economic Exposure**: The long-term risk that exchange rates will affect a firm's future competitive capability and cash flows.

### Q2. What are the key elements of Michael Porter's National Diamond Model?
**Answer**:
1. Factor Conditions
2. Demand Conditions
3. Related and Supporting Industries
4. Firm Strategy, Structure, and Rivalry.
