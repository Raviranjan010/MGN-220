# Key Glossary & Terminology Definitions

- **Ad Valorem Tariff**: A tariff levied as a fixed percentage of the value of the imported good.
- **Bill of Lading**: Legal cargo receipt and title document issued by a ocean/air carrier.
- **Expatriate**: An employee sent by a firm to work in a foreign subsidiary.
- **Hedging**: Action taken to minimize foreign exchange rate risks using derivatives (forwards, options).
- **Incoterms**: 11 standardized rules detailing allocation of risk, cost, and delivery in international transport.
- **Dumping**: Selling exported goods below cost of production to capture market share.
- **Joint Venture**: A cooperative business entity established by two or more independent parent firms.
