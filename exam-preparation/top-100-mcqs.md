# Top 100 Solved MCQs (Full Syllabus Practice)

### Q1. Which WTO principle states that domestic and imported goods should be treated equally?
- (A) Most Favored Nation (MFN)
- (B) National Treatment
- (C) Reciprocity
- (D) Quantitative Restriction
* **Correct Answer**: (B) National Treatment

### Q2. Which staffing policy uses host-country nationals to manage local subsidiaries?
- (A) Ethnocentric
- (B) Polycentric
- (C) Geocentric
- (D) Regiocentric
* **Correct Answer**: (B) Polycentric

### Q3. A contract to exchange currency in 90 days at a rate locked in today is a:
- (A) Spot Contract
- (B) Forward Contract
- (C) Swap Contract
- (D) Currency option
* **Correct Answer**: (B) Forward Contract

### Q4. The J-Curve effect explains:
- (A) The relationship between tariff rates and trade tax revenue.
- (B) The short-term worsening and long-term improvement of trade balances after currency depreciation.
- (C) The optimal allocation of factors under factor proportions theory.
- (D) The expansion path of regional integration levels.
* **Correct Answer**: (B) The short-term worsening and long-term improvement of trade balances after currency depreciation.
