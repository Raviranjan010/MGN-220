# Formula and Trade Theory Cheat Sheet

## 1. Opportunity Cost Calculation (Comparative Advantage)
To calculate the opportunity cost of producing Good X:
$$\text{Opportunity Cost of 1 unit of X} = \frac{\text{Labor hours required for 1 unit of Y}}{\text{Labor hours required for 1 unit of X}}$$
*Rule*: Specialize in the good with the **lowest** opportunity cost.

## 2. Exchange Rate Calculations
- **Bid-Ask Spread**:
  $$\text{Spread \%} = \frac{\text{Ask Price} - \text{Bid Price}}{\text{Ask Price}} \times 100$$
- **Forward Premium/Discount**:
  $$\text{Premium/Discount \% (Annualized)} = \frac{\text{Forward Rate} - \text{Spot Rate}}{\text{Spot Rate}} \times \frac{360}{\text{Forward Days}} \times 100$$
