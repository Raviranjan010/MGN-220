# Solved PYQ 2024 (LPU End-Term Examination)

### Section A (Short Compulsory Questions - 2 Marks Each)
#### Q1: Define 'Incoterms'.
*Answer*: Standardized trade terms defined by the ICC showing risk and cost allocation during international transport.

#### Q2: State Heckscher-Ohlin Theory.
*Answer*: Countries export goods utilizing their abundant and cheap factors of production intensively.

---

### Section B (Medium Length Questions - 5 Marks Each)
#### Q3: Contrast IMF and World Bank objectives.
*Answer*: The IMF resolves short-term balance-of-payments issues to secure monetary stability. The World Bank funds long-term infrastructure and developmental projects.
