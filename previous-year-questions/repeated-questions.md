# Repeated & Expected Questions (LPU End-Term Analysis)

Analysis of past 5 years of MGN220 papers shows these 5 questions are repeated in almost every session:

---

## 1. Absolute vs. Comparative Advantage (CO2)
- **Question Pattern**: "Differentiate between Adam Smith's Absolute Advantage theory and David Ricardo's Comparative Advantage theory. Explain with a mathematical example."
- **Expected Marks**: 10 Marks
- **Key Focus**: Opportunity cost calculations and production capability ratios.

---

## 2. PESTLE Framework in Business Environment (CO1)
- **Question Pattern**: "Critically analyze how the Social and Political environments impact the entry decisions of an MNC. Support with a corporate example."
- **Expected Marks**: 8/10 Marks
- **Key Focus**: McDonald's menu changes in India, or cultural barriers in Europe.

---

## 3. IMF vs. World Bank Roles (CO3)
- **Question Pattern**: "Compare the operational objectives of the IMF and the World Bank. How do their financing models differ during crises?"
- **Expected Marks**: 5 or 10 Marks
- **Key Focus**: Short-term balance-of-payments issues (IMF) vs. long-term developmental infrastructure loans (World Bank).

---

## 4. Modes of Market Entry (CO4)
- **Question Pattern**: "Discuss the trade-offs of entering foreign markets via Wholly Owned Subsidiaries vs. Joint Ventures. Under what conditions is a joint venture preferred?"
- **Expected Marks**: 10 Marks
- **Key Focus**: Control, financial risk, and local market expertise transfer.

---

## 5. Staffing Policies in GHRM (CO5)
- **Question Pattern**: "What are the advantages and disadvantages of Ethnocentric, Polycentric, and Geocentric staffing models in Global Human Resource Management?"
- **Expected Marks**: 8 Marks
- **Key Focus**: Expatriate adjustment and parent-company control structures.
